The Rules

  • The jar of beans is at the plant-based booth at the Davis Farmers’ Market every Saturday (weather permitting) from 8:00-1:00 in the non-profit/community area.
  • It is free to guess (estimate) the number of beans in the jar. You can submit your guess online here: Bean Count Guess. You can update your guess by submitting another one.
  • The contest will end on December 12th. It will then take a little while to carefully count the beans.
  • The 4 guesses that are closest to the actual count will win the prizes. The winners will be notified and the results will be posted on this web site.

    Everyone will receive a message - win or lose.

The Prizes

  1. \$50 gift certificate at the Davis Food Co-op at 620 G Street.
  2. \$30 gift certificate at Trader Joe’s at 885 Russell Blvd.
  3. Cool Beans - bean cookbook by Joe Yonan, published by Ten Speed Press in 2020.
